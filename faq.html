How do I design my car?

Use the car designer tool on the home page. You can draw the body and wheels, upload images, and adjust performance settings.

When is the next race?

Races occur weekly. Check the countdown on the home page for the exact time remaining.

How are winners determined?

Winners are based on a combination of design creativity and allocated performance credits.

Can I submit multiple cars?

Each team can submit one car per race. Use unique team and car names.
